🚀 Our First Integration

Sync Google Tasks with Monday.com — Automatically

Tired of manually copying tasks between platforms? Vimea Systems offers seamless two-way sync between Google Tasks and Monday.com so your to-dos stay aligned — wherever you work.

  • ✅ Sync tasks automatically between Google Tasks and Monday.com
  • ✅ Keep due dates, descriptions, and status in sync
  • ✅ Avoid double entry and missed updates
  • ✅ Ideal for individuals and teams using both tools

🔧 How It Works

  1. Connect your accounts – Authorize Monday.com and Google
  2. Choose your boards and task lists – Select what to sync
  3. Set your preferences – One-way or two-way, real-time or interval-based
  4. Let it run – We keep everything in sync behind the scenes

🔒 Private, Secure, and Reliable

Your credentials are encrypted, and we never store your tasks beyond what’s necessary to sync them. Built on secure cloud infrastructure.
